Educational robotics kits

Prototype and give life to your ideas.

Hardware tailored to your Autonomous Mobile Robots, Unmanned Ground Vehicles, Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, or robotics arm projects. The kits are easy to assemble, use, and maintain.

The kits are shipped with sample codes showcasing potential applications. DIfferent models are avialable, ideal for students, hobbyists, and professionals.

Next

Base model

Arduino-based mobile robot.

Kit for an Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) controlled by an Arduino microcontroller. This kit offers a wide range of applications such as obstacle avoidance, line following, smoke detection. Sensors can be added to the kit to extend its functionalities.

Advanced model

Raspberry-pi-4-based robot.

Kit for an Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) controlled by a Raspberry pi 4 microcontroller. This kit is an upgrade of the base model and is capable of autonomous navigation using the Simultaneous Localization And Mapping (SLAM) algorithms available in the Robot Operating System (ROS) 2 framework.
